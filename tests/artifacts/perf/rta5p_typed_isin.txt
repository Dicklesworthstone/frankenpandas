================================================================================

eydcr fourth-wave op. isin_apply_column already had an Int64 dense-bitset path
(f7201) but a Float64 column fell to the generic `col.values()` Vec<Scalar>
materialization + per-element idx.contains loop.

LEVER (one cohesive typed-membership probe):
  1. Typed Float64 branch: read the f64 + validity slices and probe membership
     directly. A present slot (validity set AND datum not NaN) is probed by the
     EXACT same idx.contains over a stack Scalar::Float64 — no Vec<Scalar>
     materialization; every other slot's membership is idx.contains's missing arm,
     missing_kinds.contains(NaN), precomputed once.
  2. IsinIndex sets HashSet -> FxHashSet: contains() is a hot per-element probe and
     the residual after removing materialization was SipHash over the u64 float
     bits. FxHash gives identical membership with a much cheaper hash.
